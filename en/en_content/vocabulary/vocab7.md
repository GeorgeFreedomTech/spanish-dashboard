## Actions:
- to shuffle – barajar
- to roll / throw (dice) – lanzar / tirar
- to move – mover
- to win – ganar
- to exhaust / tap (a card) – agotar

- It's your turn. – Es tu turno.

- Your character is exhausted, you cannot perform an action. – Tu personaje está agotado, no puedes hacer una acción.
- How many resource tokens do you have left? – ¿Cuántos tokens de recursos te quedan?
